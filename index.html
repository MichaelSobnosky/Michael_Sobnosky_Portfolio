EC 410 Empirical Project 2

This projects takes a look at how class size affects short term and long term test score results for students. By analyzing data and reviewing professional economic papers, I conclude that hiring more teachers and reducing class sizes will improve both short-term test scores and long-term student outcomes. While new teachers may initially have less experience, overall performance will still improve. Smaller class sizes lead to higher future earnings, increased high school completion, and higher college attendance rates, making this a beneficial investment.
